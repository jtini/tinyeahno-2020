Tin Yeah No

Colophon

Jeremy Tinianow designed and developed this site. “Tin Yeah No” is how you pronounce his last name.

This site was designed in Figma and built with Gatsby. It's hosted on Netlify.

The big beautiful serif display face is called Moret and was designed by Jamie Chang. Most of the type is set in National 2 from Klim Type Foundry. The little monospaced bits use ETC Trispace from Etcetera Type Company.
